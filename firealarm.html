Fire alarm, extinguisher and security.

1. Smoke and Heat detectors.

Smoke and heat detectors provide early fire detection by sensing smoke particles or rising temperatures and triggering an alarm for immediate action.

2. Control Panal

Bosch Control Panel

The Bosch Solution 2000 is a reliable, hard-wired entry-level home and small business security intrusion panel. It features support for up to 8 wired security zones and up to 16 unique user codes. While it provides robust wired protection, it does not support wireless sensors or detectors natively.

Honey Morley Lite

The Honeywell Morley Lite is a compact, high-performance fire alarm control panel designed specifically to meet the addressability and safety requirements of small to medium-sized commercial and residential buildings.

3. Fire Extinguishers.

Available from 1 kg to 50 kg.

Fire extinguishers are portable safety tools used to suppress small, contained fires before they spread into life-threatening emergencies. They work by removing at least one element of the "fire tetrahedron": heat, oxygen, fuel, or the chemical chain reaction.
